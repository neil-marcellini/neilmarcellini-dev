Fitdigits

About

Fitdigits is a fitness tracking platform with a web app and mobile apps such as iCardio and iRunner. They specialize in heart rate training, personalized workouts, and coaching to help users achieve their fitness goals.

My Experience

I started working at Fitdigits in May 2020 as a summer job. It was my first programming job and I had a lot to learn. Over the course of the summer I picked up Javascript, Angular, the older Angular JS, and I improved my HTML and CSS skills. I worked on the front end of their web app, and I mostly upgraded pages from Angular JS to Angular in order to help modernize the app. It was great experience to work with a small team of developers and we made the best of it despite the isolation of the pandemic.

Some Notable Contributions

Upgraded numerous pages from Angular JS to Angular

Improved data validation on forms for goals and manual entry of workouts.

Suggested and implemented a new system for localization using ngx-translate and added localization to numerous pages.

Added or fixed dark mode on upgraded pages.

Styled various graphs and making them look good on mobile.
