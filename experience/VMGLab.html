VMG Lab

About

VMG Lab is an Apple Watch app that connects to an ultrasonic wind sensor to measure VMG and sailing efficiency. It is designed to be a small, sharp tool to improve sailing performance. The app provides real-time feedback on sailing efficiency, helping sailors optimize their VMG (Velocity Made Good) and make better tactical decisions on the water.
